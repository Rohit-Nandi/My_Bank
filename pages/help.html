Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions.

Why is my transaction pending?

Transactions may take up to 24 hours. Please wait and check back later.

How can I transfer money to another account?

Go to the "Transfer" section in your dashboard, enter the recipient's account details, amount, and confirm using the OTP sent to your phone.

How can I check my transaction history?

Log in to your account and go to the "Transactions" tab to view recent and past activity.

How can I update my phone number or email address?

After logging in, go to the "Profile Settings" page and click "Edit Contact Info". Enter your new details and confirm using an OTP.
